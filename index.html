My Journey into Tech

From Curiosity to Coding

Meet Valerie-Anne, a young woman who recently decided to embark on a new and exiciting journey into the world of technology and coding.She had always been curious about how websites and applications worked, but never imagined she would start learning how to build them herself.

The first steps

Valerie-Anne began by learning the basics of html, the foundation of web development. She created her first webpage, adding headings, paragraphs, and even formatting her text using bold, italic, and underline elements.

Every new tag she learned broughta sense of accomplishment. She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is eager to explore more. Next on her list: learning more HTML elements and CSS to style her web pages!

Challenges and Growth

The journey wasn't always easy. Valerie-Anne encountered errors, bugs, and challenges that tested her patience. But she never gave up. She knew that with every mistake, she was one step closer to becoming a confident coder. At the end of this program, Valerie-Anne can build simple web pages and will slowly venture into JavaScript to make her projects interactive.

The Road Ahead

Valerie-Anne knows that the road to mastering tech is long, but she is excited for the future. She's even started sharing her progress on social media to inspire others, especially other women, to dive into the world of coding.

Conclusion

Valerie-Anne's journey is just the beginning. With passion, determination, and the ability to never stop learning, she knows that the possibilities in tech are endless.
